The Fault in Our Stars

By John Green

Get "The Fault in Our Stars" on Amazon.com

This story focuses on a teenage girl named Hazel and her experience living with thyroid cancer and finding love in an unexpected way. Her lover, Augustus, also has cancer, and they explore the wonders of life together while they have time. They eventually meet Hazel's favorite writer, but he is not what they expected, which changes Hazel's view on life in a few ways.

Looking for Alaska

By John Green

Get "Looking for Alaska" on Amazon.com

This story focuses on a boy named Miles, or "Pudge," and his enrollment in a new school for new experiences. He meets a girl named Alaska who sends him and his roommate, "the Colonel," on a journey through her past and guilt. While they explore her story, they go through an adventure of escapes, confusion, and emotions to find out the truth and story of Alaska.

Turtles All the Way Down

By John Green

Get "Turtles All the Way Down" on Amazon.com

This story focuses on a girl named Aza Holmes, a teenager who struggles with OCD and anxiety. She struggles to live her life the way she wants when her mind takes over. A local billionaire goes missing, leaving his fortune and property to his children, but his knowabouts are still a mystery. Aza's friend, Daisey, joins Aza on an adventure and investigation into the billionaire's wherabouts, in hopes of receiving the reward for his location.

Paper Towns

By John Green

Get "Paper Towns" on Amazon.com

This story focuses on a boy named Quentin, a teenager who is known for his smarts and geekiness. He is drawn to a girl named Margo, who is his neighbor. Margo is a mystery to uncover within her feelings and her evenutal whereabouts. Margo goes missing and leaves clues and hints for Quentin to uncover and solve to find her.

An Abundance of Katherines

By John Green

Get "An Abundance of Katherines" on Amazon.com

This story focuses on Colin, a boy who is known for his prodigy skills and genius mind. Colin has a theme of dating girls, all with the same name of Katherine, and all who have dumped him. Colin's friend, Hassan, takes Colin on a road trip in hopes of changing Colin's outlook on love and life. He learns to be happy being single and alone in life through his adventure with Hassan.

Will Grayson, Will Grayson

By John Green

Get "Will Grayson, Will Grayson" on Amazon.com

This story focuses on two teenagers with the same name, Will Grayson. While they share the same name, they experience very different lives that are opposites of each other. The two Will Grayson's meet and their lives become intertwined.

The Anthropocene Reviewed

By John Green

Get "The Anthropocene Reviewed" on Amazon.com

This book is a bit different from other John Green books as it focuses on a collection of essays that center around different aspects of the human planet. It focuses on shared human experience to explain our planet and biodiversity. It also explored the faults and merits of ourselves as humans in our world.
