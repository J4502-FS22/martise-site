7 Coffee Shops in St. Louis That I Need to Visit

As a born-and-raised St. Louis resident, I have explored many aspects of the city. One aspect I need more time exploring is the coffee scene of the St. Louis area. Here is a list of the coffee shops in St. Louis that I need to visit soon.

Blueprint Coffee

6225 Delmar Blvd
St. Louis, MO
63130
(314) 266-6808

Visit Blueprint Coffee's website

Blueprint Coffee is a coffee shop that provides specialty roasted coffee to producers. The shop specially selects their coffees from a wide variety to ensure customer satisfaction.

Kaldi's Coffee

52 N Euclid Ave
St. Louis, MO
63108
(314) 312-2060

Visit Kaldi Coffee's website

Kaldi's Coffee prides themselves on leading coffee companies with specialty blends and roasts. The shop wants to create an experience for their customers through their coffee and brand.

Maypop Coffee & Garden Shop

803 Marshall Ave
Webster Groves, MO
63119
(314) 764-2140

Visit Maypop Coffee & Garden Shop's website

Maypop Coffee & Garden Shop provides both coffee and plants to their customers. The shop also believe in biodiversity and modern ease with living spaces.

Fiddlehead Fern Café

4066 Russell Blvd
St. Louis, MO
63110
(314) 300-8111

Visit Fiddlehead Fern Café's website

Fiddlehead Fern Café uses ingrediants that are both seasonal and botanical based in their coffee products. The shop also uses local inspiration and the music of fiddles on the weekend to grow their brand with customers.

The Mud House

2101 Cherokee St
St. Louis, MO
63118
(314) 776-6599

Visit The Mud House's website

The Mud House brands itself as a hip, open, friendly shop to all customers. The shop has pride in its coffee and meal options that are made from scratch daily.

Picasso's Coffee House

1650 Beale St #155
Saint Charles, MO
63303
(314) 410-8348

Visit Picasso's Coffee House's website

Picasso's Coffee House uses local micro-roasted ingredients in their coffees. The shop supports the local community through economic and relational efforts.

Coma Coffee

1034 S Brentwood Blvd
Richmond Heights, MO
63117
(314) 250-1042

Visit Coma Coffee's website

Coma Coffee is known for their high quality coffees from around the world. The shop also believes in the ways that coffee can bring people together.
